How to Set Up Your Calendly

Step 1: Login to Calendly
Go to the Calendly website and log in using your credentials. If you do not have an account, you will need to sign up.
Step 2: Create an Event
Once logged in, navigate to the dashboard. Click on "Event Types" on the top menu. Then, click on "New Event Type" and choose your event type (One-on-One or Group). Fill in the necessary details for your event, such as event name, location, duration, and description.
Step 3: Get Your Event URL
After creating your event, Calendly will provide you with a unique URL for your event. This URL can be shared with others to schedule the event. You can find this URL on the event details page.
Step 4: Obtain Your Personal Access Token
To integrate Calendly with other applications, you'll need a personal access token. Go to your Account Settings by clicking on your profile at the bottom left corner. Select "Integrations" from the menu. Scroll down to "API & Webhooks" and click on "Personal Access Tokens". Click on "New Token", give it a name, and then copy the generated token. Keep this token secure as it provides access to your Calendly account.

For more detailed instructions and additional settings, please refer to the Calendly Help Center.
