How to Set Up Your Calendly

Note: Your Tutor account can only be activated after you have successfully connected your Calendly account. Please follow the steps below to set up your Calendly account and integrate it with your tutor dashboard.
Step 1: Register for a Calendly Account
Go to the Calendly website and click on "Sign Up" to create a new account. Fill in the required information and follow the prompts to complete the registration process.
Step 2: Login to Calendly
Once you have registered, log in using your credentials.
Step 3: Create an Event
After logging in, navigate to the dashboard. Click on "Event Types" on the top menu. Then, click on "New Event Type" and choose your event type (One-on-One). Fill in the necessary details for your event, such as event name, location, duration, and description.
Step 4: Get Your Event URL
After creating your event, Calendly will provide you with a unique URL for your event. This URL can be shared with others to schedule the event. You can find this URL on the event details page.

If you already have your Event URL, you can add it now:

Add Calendly Event URL
Step 5: Connect Your Calendly Account
To integrate Calendly with your tutor dashboard, click on the "Connect Calendly" button on the dashboard page. Follow the instructions to authorize the connection between your tutor account and Calendly.

If you have already set up your Calendly account, you can simply connect it now:

Connect Calendly

For more detailed instructions and additional settings, please refer to the Calendly Help Center.
